W05 Learning Activity: Sharing the Gospel Online

What I learned

From “Waiting on the Road to Damascus” by President Dieter F. Uchtdorf, I learned that a testimony does not always come through a dramatic spiritual experience. Sometimes it comes little by little as we move forward with faith, listen to the Spirit, serve others, and share the gospel. We should not wait for a miracle before believing. Instead, we should trust God and take small steps of faith every day.

How I can share the gospel online

From “How to Be Yourself While Sharing the Gospel Online,” I learned that I can share the gospel in a natural and sincere way. I can build relationships, be authentic, share uplifting messages, talk about the Savior, ask questions, and be a good example online. I do not need to be perfect. I just need to be honest and let others see how the gospel helps me in my life.

Personal reflection

I can share the gospel online by being myself and using social media in a positive way. For example, I can share a scripture, a quote, or a personal experience that inspires me. I can also support others with kind comments and messages. Small actions can make a big difference and help others feel God’s love.
